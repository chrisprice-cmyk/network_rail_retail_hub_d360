## Getting Support

### Brix Owner

The current brix owner, will be defined within the cumulusci.yml file, next to qbrix_owner_name.

### Qverse Knowledge and myTrailhead

The QVerse Knowledge Portal (https://qlabs-org.my.site.com/qverse/s/) can be accessed with an Okta login. It contains knowledge base articles and the ability to view the current Trust page (https://qlabs-org.my.site.com/qverse/s/trust) which details any current known issues.

Further brix building training can be found on the QLABS myTrailhead site https://qlabs.my.trailhead.com/trails

### Slack Channel

Get support in Slack on the NextGen Demos - External Channel (#nextgendemos-dev-external) here: https://salesforce.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C039WDURCSH
